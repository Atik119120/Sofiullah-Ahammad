About

Sofiullah Ahammad (professionally known as Atik Ahmed) is a Bangladeshi photographer, cinematographer, motion designer and web developer based in Rajshahi, Bangladesh. He is known for his black-classic cinematic style in portrait, street, minimal and product photography. Since 2022 he has worked across commissioned projects and personal visual language development — moody lighting, strong contrast and film-inspired composition. He is the founder of the creative community Alokchitra, which runs photo meets, learning sessions and storytelling experiments.
