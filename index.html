PROFILE
Sofiullah Ahammad
Photographer • Cinematographer • Motion Designer

Sofiullah Ahammad is a Bangladeshi photographer, cinematographer and motion designer known for his cinematic black–classic visual style. His work blends emotional storytelling, minimal composition and controlled lighting — shaping visuals that feel atmospheric and modern.

Beginning his creative journey in 2022, he expanded into motion graphics and modern web design while shaping a film-inspired aesthetic. He also founded Alokchitra, a creative community helping young visual artists grow through workshops, photo meets and collaborative projects focused on visual storytelling and craft.

His portfolio spans portrait, minimal, street, product and landscape photography — unified by strong contrast, deep mood, and intentional cinematic framing.
