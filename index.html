About

Sofiullah Ahammad (professionally known as Atik Ahmed) is a Bangladeshi photographer, cinematographer and motion designer based in Rajshahi. He develops a black-classic cinematic visual language — moody lighting, strong contrast and filmic composition. He founded Alokchitra, a creative community for photographers and visual storytellers.
